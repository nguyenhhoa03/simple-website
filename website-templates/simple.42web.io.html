About Simple Templates

Simple Templates is a curated collection of beautiful, responsive website templates built with modern web technologies. Our mission is to provide developers and designers with high-quality, open-source templates that are easy to use and customize.

All templates are carefully crafted with attention to detail, following web standards and best practices. They're designed to be lightweight, fast-loading, and SEO-friendly, giving you a solid foundation for your next web project.

Every template is released under the GNU General Public License v3, ensuring they remain free and open source for everyone. Whether you're building a personal portfolio, business website, or learning web development, our templates provide the perfect starting point.
